Our Events

Upcoming Events

Tech Hackathon 2025

Join us for an intense 48-hour coding event where tech enthusiasts solve real-world problems. Teams will compete to build the best tech solutions for given challenges.

This year, the hackathon focuses on sustainability, encouraging participants to create tools that address global environmental concerns. The event includes mentoring sessions with tech experts, networking opportunities, and fun activities to keep the energy up.

Participants will be provided with meals, a work desk, and access to necessary tech tools. The top 3 teams will win prizes worth ₹1 lakh, along with internships at leading tech companies. Certificates will be provided to all participants. Join us to code, create, and innovate!

AI for Future

Dive into the world of Artificial Intelligence with our seminar on the future of AI and its growing impact on various industries.

Topics include machine learning, deep learning, and the ethical challenges surrounding AI adoption in the modern world. Gain insights from industry leaders and researchers on AI advancements, applications, and trends for the next decade.

The seminar will feature interactive sessions, live demonstrations of AI-powered tools, and an open Q&A segment. Discover how AI is revolutionizing healthcare, education, and manufacturing industries.

Previous Events

Cyber Security 2024

A hands-on workshop where participants explored the basics of ethical hacking and cybersecurity.

The workshop covered network vulnerabilities, data breaches, and defense techniques in an engaging and practical manner. Attendees learned how to perform penetration testing and protect systems from real-world cyber threats.

By the end, participants received certificates and gained foundational knowledge in cybersecurity. Many described it as a life-changing introduction to the field of ethical hacking!

Web Dev Bootcamp

A beginner-friendly workshop where participants created responsive websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Highlights included: creating interactive web forms, integrating APIs, and learning tips to make websites visually appealing and user-friendly. The bootcamp concluded with a showcase of the participants' projects, allowing everyone to demonstrate their creativity and technical skills.

Certificates and prizes were awarded to the top-performing participants. Feedback from attendees indicated that it was a fun and enlightening experience for all!

AR Workshop 2024

Learn the basics of Augmented Reality and build interactive AR experiences in this hands-on workshop.

This workshop introduces the fundamentals of AR, from creating immersive experiences to using various AR platforms. Participants will get to work with AR development tools and create their own AR applications.

Attendees will also explore the future of AR and how it's shaping industries such as education, retail, and entertainment. Certificates will be provided to all participants.

Cloud Computing 2024

A workshop covering the basics of cloud computing, its architecture, and its real-world applications.

Participants will gain hands-on experience with major cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud. The workshop includes building cloud-based applications and learning about the cloud infrastructure.

This event will be valuable for anyone interested in cloud technologies, especially those aiming to pursue a career in cloud engineering or DevOps. Certificates and refreshments will be provided.

IoT Workshop 2024

Explore the world of the Internet of Things (IoT) with a workshop focused on building smart devices and connecting them to the internet.

The workshop covers the basics of IoT, including how to connect sensors, actuators, and other hardware components to the internet. Participants will build their own IoT projects and explore use cases in smart homes and industries.

The event concludes with a demonstration of the IoT projects, and top projects will win exciting prizes. This workshop is a great introduction for anyone interested in the IoT field.
